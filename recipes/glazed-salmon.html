Glazed Salmon

Ingredients

  • salmon fillets
  • 4 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp sriracha
  • salt
  • pepper
  • paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to a broil (~400)
  2. Mince garlic. Pat salmon fillets dry and season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. Heat an oven safe skillet over medium high heat. Combine balsamic vinegar, honey, soy sauce, water, sriracha, and garlic to make glaze.
  4. Add salmon skin side down to skillet. Baste constantly for 3 minutes.
  5. Add skillet to oven, bake for 3 minutes, baste, then bake another 3 minutes.
